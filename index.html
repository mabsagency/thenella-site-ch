About THENELLA

THENELLA is a passionate gospel artist dedicated to creating music that inspires faith, brings hope, and leads people into authentic worship experiences.

With a voice that carries both power and tenderness, THENELLA's music blends traditional gospel roots with contemporary sounds, creating a unique worship experience that resonates with people of all ages.

Her mission is to use music as a tool for ministry, spreading the message of God's love and grace through every song and performance.
